Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Detail

Since 2017, we've helped homeowners, landlords, and businesses across Surrey and London enjoy spaces that feel genuinely cared for.

Meet Crystal

A Personal Commitment to Impeccable Standards

I'm Crystal, Founder and Director of Sheer Sparkles Ltd. I started this company in 2017 with a simple belief: everyone deserves a space that feels genuinely cared for.

What began as a local service in East Molesey has grown into one of Surrey's most trusted names in premium cleaning. We've proudly served clients across Kingston upon Thames, East and West Molesey, and the wider London boroughs for over seven years.

Every member of our team is expertly trained, fully vetted, and shares our commitment to meticulous standards. We use high-quality, eco-conscious products to ensure every space is not only beautifully clean but also safe and thoughtfully cared for.
