Our Story

A Personal Commitment to Impeccable Standards

Founded in 2017, Sheer Sparkles was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a space that feels genuinely cared for. What started as a local cleaning service in East Molesey has grown into one of Surrey's most trusted names in premium residential and commercial cleaning.

We specialise in one-off, deep, and turnaround cleans for homeowners, landlords, Airbnb hosts, and businesses. Our expertly trained team uses high-quality, eco-conscious products to deliver results that go beyond surface-level — restoring freshness, hygiene, and calm to every room we touch.

Whether you're preparing a property for new tenants, hosting your next Airbnb guests, or simply reclaiming your weekends, we bring the same meticulous care to every job — because the details are what set us apart.
